
### Charcot-Marie-Tooth Disease Type 4A (CMT4A)
### **Charcot-Marie-Tooth Disease Type 4A (CMT4A) Disease Profile****Disease Overview**
Charcot-Marie-Tooth disease type 4A (CMT4A) is a rare, genetic neurological disorder and a subtype of CMT41.2. It is a sensorimotor neuropathy that can present as demyelinating, axonal, or intermediate, meaning it affects the protective covering of nerves, the nerve fibers themselves, or both3. The condition is caused by pathogenic mutations in the GDAP1 gene1,4.**Epidemiology/Prevalence**
